About

Old Novice (Old Novice 資訊方案一人有限公司) is a Macau-based IT company founded in 2011.

We are working on mlocal.biz, which is an online directory of Macau local business. Now the website is in early stage, we only include small number of businesses with basic information (name, address, phone number). Our goal is to let people find a business by products/services.
